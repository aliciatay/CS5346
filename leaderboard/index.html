About this visualization: This animated bar chart shows how the top 20 happiest countries change over time, based on the World Happiness Report data from 2015 to 2023.

Bars represent happiness scores, with longer bars indicating higher happiness levels. Colors represent geographic regions.

Use the country filter to track a specific country's performance over time, even when it's not in the top 20.

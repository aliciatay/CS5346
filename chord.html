Feature Correlation Analysis

This chord diagram visualizes the correlations between musical characteristics and technical features in songs. The colors of the outer segments indicate whether a feature is musical (light red) or technical (light blue). The connecting chords show correlations between features, with the color and width indicating the strength and direction of the correlation. Hover over any chord to see the exact correlation value.

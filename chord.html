Hit Threshold Explained

The hit threshold controls how selective the analysis is in choosing which songs to include:

  • Higher threshold (e.g., 75%): Only includes songs that are in the top percentile for both streams and playlist appearances - focusing on the biggest hits
  • Lower threshold (e.g., 25%): Includes a broader range of songs, giving a more general view of feature relationships

For example, a 25% threshold means the analysis only includes songs that are in the top 25% for both Spotify streams AND playlist appearances.

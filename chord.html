Feature Correlations in Hit Songs

This chord diagram visualizes the correlations between different audio features in hit songs (songs that are successful on 5 or more platforms). The width of each chord represents the strength of correlation between two features, while the color indicates whether the correlation is positive (blue) or negative (red).

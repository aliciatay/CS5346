Happiness Factors Correlation Spider Chart

This visualization shows how different factors correlate with happiness scores across regions, countries and years. v3.8

Note: This visualization has two modes:

1. Correlation Mode: When multiple data points are selected (e.g., 'All' years), values range from -1.0 (strongest negative correlation) to 1.0 (strongest positive correlation).

2. Direct Values Mode: When a single data point is selected (specific country and year), the chart shows normalized factor values ranging from -1.0 to 1.0.

About this visualization: This spider chart visualizes happiness factors either as correlations or as normalized values, depending on your selection.
